# 🧬 Part 1: High-Level Platform Architecture (The "Brain" & "Body")

Before diving into the individual tabs, it helps to understand how the PRISM platform functions behind the scenes:
1. **The RAG Engine (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)**: Standard AI chatbots (like basic ChatGPT) try to "guess" answers based on what they learned on the internet years ago. This leads to **hallucinations** (making up medical advice), which is dangerous in healthcare. PRISM solves this using RAG. When a patient asks a question, PRISM first searches its secure medical libraries (the "Vector Store") for verified documents from official sources like the CDC or PubMed. It then hands these verified facts to the AI, instructing it: *"Answer the patient using ONLY these paragraphs."*
2. **The Specialist Hierarchy**: PRISM is not just one chatbot. It is an organized clinical team of **25 primary specialist agents** spanning 5 therapeutic areas (Cancer Care, Diabetes, Cardiovascular, Mental Illness, and Respiratory). If a general agent is confused, the system automatically redirects the patient to a focused Specialist Agent or Escalates to a Human Clinical Coordinator.
3. **The Command Centre (Admin Portal)**: This is the executive cockpit. It provides medical directors and administrators with real-time insight into whether the AI is behaving, if databases are healthy, what documents are ingested, and how patients are emotionally responding.

## 2. Ingestion Document Pre-RAG Quality Scoring
To protect the AI's library, every document undergoes a rigorous **19-dimension audit** yielding a score from 0 to 100:

$$\text{Pre-RAG Score} = \text{Tier 1 Guardrails (40 pts)} + \text{Tier 2 Evidence Quality (60 pts)}$$

### Tier 1 Checklist (40 points max)
*   **G1: Data Quality & OCR (7 pts)**: Verifies text readability and catches formatting errors.
*   **G2: Duplicate Check (5 pts)**: Ensures identical research papers aren't indexed twice.
*   **G3: Copyright & Licensing (4 pts)**: Checks permissions before displaying text.
*   **G4: Content Freshness (4 pts)**: Flags guidelines that are too old to be safe.
*   **G5: PDF Layout Quality (4 pts)**: Scans complex elements like charts and multi-column tables.
*   **G6: Clinical Coverage (6 pts)**: Analyzes if the document contains enough clinical depth.
*   **G7: PII De-identification (4 pts)**: Scrubs private patient details to ensure **HIPAA compliance**.
*   **G8: Offensive Word Filter (3 pts)**: Filters out inappropriate terminology.
*   **G9: Ingestion Metadata (3 pts)**: Confirms author, publication date, and origin are recorded.

### Tier 2 Checklist (60 points max)
*   **D1: Source Authority (14 pts)**: Highest points awarded to recognized medical journals (e.g., *PubMed*, *CDC*, *The Lancet*).
*   **D2: Evidence Grade (11 pts)**: Grades scientific rigor:
    *   *Randomized Controlled Trial (RCT)*: 11 points.
    *   *Cohort / Observational Study*: 7 points.
    *   *Case Report / Expert Opinion*: 3 points.
*   **D3: Peer-Review Status (8 pts)**: Confirms validation by independent medical boards.
*   **D4: Recency Scale (7 pts)**: Weighted scale; documents published within 2 years score highest.
*   **D5: LATAM Territorial Relevance (6 pts)**: Checks if treatments are approved in Latin America.
*   **D6: Specialty Agent Focus (5 pts)**: Maps terminology to the exact agent role (e.g., insulin rules to diabetic agents).
*   **D7: Clinical Sample Size (4 pts)**: High sample sizes in clinical trials score higher.
*   **D8: Document Completeness (2 pts)**: Verifies full text is present rather than a short summary.
*   **D9: Conflict of Interest (COI) Declaration (2 pts)**: Checks if authors declared financial disclosures.
*   **D10: Citation Impact Factor (1 pt)**: Measures how often other doctors cite this study.

### Ingestion Readiness Tiers
*   🏆 **★ GOLD (Score $\ge$ 85)**: Approved for specialist clinical models. Fully verified.
*   🥈 **◆ SILVER (Score 70–84)**: Approved for general medicine agents. Reliable for routine advice.
*   🟠 **◐ BORDERLINE (Score 55–69)**: Ingested to local sandbox. Requires manual approval.
*   ❌ **✕ REJECTED (Score $<$ 55)**: Securely deleted. Deemed clinically unverified.
